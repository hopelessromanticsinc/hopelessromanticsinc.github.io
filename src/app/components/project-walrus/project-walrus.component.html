What is the Walrus?

The Walrus is this character, that I've created in my illustrations. The Walrus represents the Jungian Shadow in a way. It is fear. It is guidance. One night it occured in my dream. The Walrus is the guide into my subconscious. It lives in my dreams. It does not talk, it merely feels and thinks. It is my Charon, the guide that knows it's way around desires, fear and destiny.
